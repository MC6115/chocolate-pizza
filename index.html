For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar adn stir to dissolve. Half all the figs and toss them in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the mild is just barely lukewarm. Whisk the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to the manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken dor 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture iinto a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
